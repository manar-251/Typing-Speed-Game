How to Play

  • Click Start to choose your difficulty level (Easy, Medium, Hard).
  • After selecting a level, click Start Game to begin.
  • A sentence will appear at the top of the screen.
  • Type the sentence exactly as shown in the text area below.
  • You win if you type it correctly before the time runs out!
  • If you run out of time or make too many mistakes, you lose.
  • Click Restart to try again.
